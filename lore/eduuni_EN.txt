Historical Overview of Granbell and Tyeria
________________


Granbell
________________


■ Before the Rise

During the era when the Apollon Civilization Sphere and the Dominion (forces led by Celia Dominicus) vied for supremacy, the Granbell planetary system already existed as an economic sphere boasting the second-largest GDP in space. However, at the time, it was merely a presence overshadowed by Apollon and the Dominion, and its true power never emerged onto the main stage.

Granbell deliberately observed the clash of the two giants — the Apollon-Dominion War — from the sidelines. Without suffering direct devastation, it continuously built up shrewd political and economic strategies beneath the surface.


■ Rise and Establishment of an Economic Empire

In the chaotic period following the war's end, Granbell rose to prominence in one stroke. While other forces were exhausted and collapsing, Granbell's GDP rapidly swelled, finally achieving the largest economic scale in space at 15 quadrillion dollars. It completely surpassed the influence of the former great powers, Apollon and the Dominion.

The backdrop of this growth included the following:
- Independent development of advanced technology
- Establishment of ultra-high-efficiency economic systems
- Expansion of commercial sphere through trade and diplomatic partnerships with numerous small and medium planetary systems
- Independent development of interdimensional (multiverse) technology

Granbell's dominance was primarily economic, and compared to other forces, it was culturally flexible, forming a unique civilization sphere centered on commercial activity. Many small and medium forces formed alliances with Granbell and sought its protection.


■ Multiverse Pioneering

What Granbell focused on most was the pioneering of the multiverse (multi-dimensional universe). Utilizing technology that transcended dimensions, it achieved expansion into different universes and dimensions. It incorporated energy sources and resources from other dimensions, driving repeated technological innovations on that foundation. As a result, the economic gap with other civilization spheres widened ever further.

"The power to transcend dimensions, in our hands." — Granbell's leader spoke these words, extending the commercial empire beyond the bounds of dimension.


■ Arms Supply to Evatron

While maintaining nominal neutrality, Granbell secretly provided cutting-edge weaponry to Evatron during its war to subjugate the Celians (remnant forces of Celia).
- Higher-dimensional energy weapons
- Quantum-controlled missile systems
- Gravity Collapse Warhead — causes dimensional distortion, destroying vast areas

Through this arms supply, Evatron's military power was dramatically strengthened, and the Celians' main stronghold, Erios Base, was completely annihilated. Through this cooperative relationship, Granbell further expanded its influence across all of space.


■ Granbell's Assessment of the Celia Era

Master Quincias, Granbell's early leader, left behind words highly praising the collapsed Dominion's Celia era.

"The Celia era was the most successful era in space history. In that era, technological innovation and social order were in perfect harmony. The era she lived in was the golden age before Granbell reached its peak."

This statement not only prompted a reassessment of the Celia era but also showed that Granbell positioned itself as its successor.


■ Current Status

- Space Ranking: 1st
- Total GDP: Accounts for approximately 25% of all space
- Capital: Alda City (birthplace of interdimensional communication technology, center of quantum economics)
- Current Leader: President Arzen Carline
- Scope of Influence: Spanning economy, culture, education, and military

Alda City is known as the most developed city in space, and the Alda Prime Hall, designed utilizing interdimensional distortions, can accommodate tens of thousands of people at once.

The first Universal Federation Summit was held in Granbell's capital, Alda City. President Arzen Carline delivered a speech stating that "transcending conflict and finding the path to coexistence and prosperity is our responsibility to the next generation." On the other hand, concerns have been raised from other forces that Granbell's overwhelming economic power "may lead to economic subordination," and its position as the hegemonic power of space constantly carries underlying tension.


________________


Tyeria
________________


■ Formation and Characteristics

Tyeria developed as a force considered to possess the pinnacle of military technology in space. In contrast to Granbell, which swept across space through economic power, Tyeria established its own unique mode of domination based on overwhelming military force and a space-scale defense network.

Its powerful fleet and exceptional tactical prowess are known throughout space, and it is considered impossible for any other force to completely surpass Tyeria's military power.


■ Export of Military Technology and Expansion of Economic Foundation

Initially, Tyeria's economic foundation was specialized in military affairs, but in recent years, amid competition with other forces, it has actively strengthened the export of military technology, leveraging it as a major economic foundation. Through the provision of defense technology, it holds influence over many forces and plays an important role in the security order of space.


■ Position at the Universal Federation Summit

At the first Universal Federation Summit, Tyeria's Supreme Commander Graymond Hauser strongly justified his nation's military power.

"Without military power, peace cannot be protected. Without strength, order cannot be maintained."

This statement drew fierce opposition from Queen Liana of Elyseon, who countered that "military expansion only invites further conflict," bringing the Tyeria-Elyseon rivalry to the surface.

Tension also arose in relations with Granbell's President Arzen. Tyeria maintained a balance-of-power policy through military force against Granbell's economic dominance, and Graymond showed a skeptical stance toward Arzen's calls for "coexistence," stating that "whether I trust those words depends on seeing concrete actions."

Upon concluding the summit, Graymond, while内心 engraving the necessity for cooperation beyond military power, resolved to prioritize Tyeria's security above all else.


■ Current Status

- Space Ranking: 3rd (after 1st Granbell and 2nd Elyseon)
- Greatest Strength: The strongest military power in space and a space-scale defense network
- Current Leader: Supreme Commander Graymond Hauser
- Economic Foundation: Export of defense technology

Researchers of space point out that the current relationship between Granbell and Tyeria harbors the danger of a "Thucydides Trap" similar to the former Apollon-Dominion relationship. The dynamic of Tyeria attempting to maintain balance through military power against Granbell's overwhelming economic strength constantly carries the potential for latent conflict between the two forces.

Relationship Between the Two Forces and Their Position in Space
________________


Granbell and Tyeria stand at the apex of economy and military respectively in the current space order, yet their relationship is a mixture of cooperation and tension.

A proposal to prevent large-scale wars through a framework of economic interdependence centered on Granbell was placed on the agenda at the first Universal Federation Summit, but whether Tyeria will fully accept it remains uncertain.

As space becomes increasingly multipolar, there is no doubt that the Granbell-Tyeria relationship will greatly shape the future space order.

Dominion / Selinopolis
________________


■ Origin: From Gigapolis to Selinopolis

The super-mega city "Gigapolis" that existed in the E16 star system — a city where over 200,000 corporate states competed and GDP reached 14 quadrillion dollars — was the mother entity of what would later become the Dominion. Celia Dominicus seized control of Gigapolis through fierce conflict with the corporate coalition ZAMLT and established her dominion.

After establishing control, Celia rapidly grew the GDP to 25 quadrillion dollars and renamed the city "Selinopolis." She ascended as the "Empress of the New Dimension" and declared the dawn of a new era aiming for space supremacy.

"Gigapolis is a city of the past. From here, a new future will be carved by our hands."


■ Dimensional Energy Technology and Female-Led Society

The greatest features of Selinopolis were the commercialization of dimensional energy technology and a female-led social structure.

- Established the super-corporate state Phovos, dominating the economy entirely around dimensional energy technology
- Achieved an overwhelming female leadership society with 90% of key executives being women
- Developed education, healthcare, and welfare systems, establishing an equal social foundation

Through this unique system, Selinopolis came to wield enormous influence across all of space, advancing diplomatic and trade expansion with other galaxies aiming to build an intergalactic exchange zone.


■ Alliance with Evatron

When the expanding Selinopolis faced the formidable threat of the Apollon Civilization Sphere, Celia sought an alliance with Evatron, which possessed a male-chauvinist culture. Despite significant cultural opposition, diplomat Elliott Strass secured Evatron's alliance through negotiations, establishing an anti-Apollon posture. With the alliance's formation, Selinopolis developed further, reaching a GDP of 81 quadrillion dollars. The city was also renamed "Dominion."


■ Great War with Apollon and Collapse

The conflict with Apollon ultimately escalated into full-scale war. When Apollon fired the "Centaurus Laser," Celia responded with the "G4 Phantom Pulse." After a fierce war of attrition, Celia attacked and destroyed Apollon's core planet "Apollon Centralis" with the Venom Fleet, succeeding in defeating Ronan Arthur.

However, the cost of victory was immense.
- Dominion's GDP: Plummeted from 81 quadrillion dollars to 23 quadrillion dollars
- Apollon's GDP: Plummeted from 125 quadrillion dollars to 32 quadrillion dollars

Both civilizations suffered decisive damage, and in the post-war chaos, Celia's grip on power rapidly disintegrated.


■ Absorption by Evatron and Present

Former ally Evatron did not let the war-weary Dominion slip by. Backed by economic leverage and military force, it purchased and absorbed Dominion's vast star systems one after another, finally expelling and deposing Celia. Evatron executive Vylon Deax was appointed as Dominion's ruler, and Evatron's culture and values permeated all of Dominion.

In response, Celia fled to the Andromeda Galaxy. The "Celians," who inherited Celia's ideals, waged a resistance movement, but were suppressed by Evatron, which had received weapons from Granbell.

Currently, Dominion's territory is under Evatron control as the E16 System. Remnant Celians continue underground activities, and their ideals have not completely disappeared.


________________


Apollon Civilization Sphere
________________


■ Characteristics and Rise

The Apollon Civilization Sphere was a massive civilization renowned throughout the galaxy for its overwhelming economic scale and powerful military organization, the "Knights of Apollon." At its peak, its GDP reached 105 quadrillion dollars, far surpassing Selinopolis's 70 quadrillion dollars at the time.

Based on the core planet "Apollon Centralis," all military forces were mobilized from there. The Knights were known for their discipline and loyalty backed by martial strength, boasting one of the most formidable fighting forces in space.

Leader: Ronan Arthur (a heroic figure standing at the apex of the Knights)


■ Conflict with Celia and the Great War

The conflict surfaced when Selinopolis, led by Celia, accepted an emissary to Apollon Centralis. Apollon, through Ronan, proposed an alliance and coexistence with Celia, but Celia refused. When Apollon fired the "Centaurus Laser" (2.6 years to reach), Celia countered with the "G4 Phantom Pulse" (2.3 years to reach), and Ronan Arthur declared war.

In the final phase, Celia's Venom Fleet conquered and shattered Apollon Centralis, and Ronan Arthur fell in battle. Apollon's leadership collapsed, and the civilization suffered devastating damage.

- Peak GDP: 125 quadrillion dollars → Plummeted to 32 quadrillion dollars after the war


■ Present

After Ronan's death, successors of the Apollon Civilization Sphere have attempted reconstruction, but its former glory remains lost. It has now fallen from the ranks of space's major forces, and its past influence no longer exists. The essential cause of the war — the clash of convictions between Ronan and Celia — continues to be spoken of throughout space, etched in history as the collision between Apollon loyalty and the desire for domination.


________________


Evatron
________________


■ Origin and Culture

Evatron is a galaxy founded on a male-chauvinist culture spanning generations. It positioned strength and warfare as the foundation of order, and women's social status was extremely low. At the time of first contact with Celia, its GDP was 33 quadrillion dollars, but its combat power and political influence far exceeded its economic scale.

First Leader: Grim Dargos


■ Relationship with Selinopolis and Dominion

Celia established exchange and alliance with Evatron on the condition of providing dimensional energy technology. Their cultural values were polar opposites, but for Evatron, dimensional energy technology was indispensable for economic development, making the alliance pragmatically beneficial for both sides.

In the Great War with Apollon, it fought on the Celia-Dominion side. Evatron's elite forces (Commander Maxim Verd) supported Dominion's defense, saving the war situation multiple times.


■ Dominion Absorption and Establishment of Control

After the war, as Celia's rule weakened, Evatron seized the opportunity. Through economic acquisition and military pressure, it absorbed Dominion's vast star systems one after another. After expelling and deposing Celia, it appointed executive Vylon Deax as Dominion's ruler.

Evatron attempted to permeate its culture and values throughout all of Dominion, but this provoked fierce resistance from residents who had experienced the freedom and equality of Celia's era. Riots and protests occurred almost daily, and security continued to deteriorate.


■ Suppression of Celians and Cooperation with Granbell

When the Celians — Celia's remnant forces opposing Evatron's rule — launched armed resistance, Evatron requested cutting-edge weaponry from Granbell. The weapons Granbell provided, including Gravity Collapse Warheads, completely annihilated the Celians' main stronghold, "Erios Base," and the rebellion was suppressed.


■ Present

- Space Ranking: 14th (a significant decline from former Dominion dominance)
- Major Challenges: Economic and social reconstruction of the E16 System, competitive gap with Granbell and Elyseon, addressing the underground Celians movement
- Advancing reconstruction plans centered on Evapolis in the E16 System, but facing severe shortages of funds and personnel

Evatron has now completely lost its former prestige as Dominion's ruler and is navigating its position in a multipolar space order.


________________


Elyseon
________________


■ Characteristics and Role

Elyseon is a force leading all of space in medical technology and environmental regeneration technology, and it enjoys broad support from many small civilizations through its peaceful diplomatic posture and technical assistance. Unlike Granbell, it places "the preservation and regeneration of life" at the foundation of its civilization, rather than dominance or economic superiority.

- Leader: Queen Liana Solis
- Specialties: Medical technology, environmental regeneration technology, life sciences


■ Position at the Universal Federation Summit

At the first Universal Federation Summit, Elyseon directly opposed Tyeria's military expansion posture. Queen Liana's words — "Military expansion only invites further conflict. We should build peace through cooperation and technology sharing" — created a rift with Tyeria's Supreme Commander Graymond.

Along with Fallujah, Elyseon strongly supported the joint space exploration project proposed by Dioclenis.

Upon concluding the summit, Liana affirmed her mission to reconstruct all of space through Elyseon's medical and environmental technology, and resolved to bridge the values gap with other forces.


■ Present

- Space Ranking: 2nd
- Strengths: Unrivaled in medical technology and environmental regeneration technology
- Diplomatic Posture: Pacifism, promotion of technology sharing
- Potential Friction: It has been noted that Elyseon's growing economic influence through technological innovation could clash with Fallujah's cultural leadership


________________


Fallujah
________________


■ Characteristics and Role

While economically inferior to other top-ranking forces, Fallujah is unparalleled in its cultural influence and diplomatic power, which connect diverse civilizations across space. It leads inter-civilizational exchange throughout space and functions as a mediator between opposing forces.

- Leader: Council Representative Madris Carnel
- Specialties: Cultural exchange, diplomacy and mediation


■ Position at the Universal Federation Summit

At the first Universal Federation Summit, Fallujah expressed concern that Granbell's overwhelming economic power "might drive other forces into subservient positions." This, combined with Tyeria's skeptical statement, sent ripples through the assembly.

Fallujah expressed support, along with Elyseon, for Dioclenis's joint space exploration proposal. Throughout the summit, it consistently advocated for "coexistence beyond conflict."

Madris believes that "the power of culture will be the key that binds all of space together" and has begun drafting concrete plans for strengthening cultural exchange.


■ Present

- Space Ranking: 4th
- Strengths: Cultural influence and bridging role between forces
- Challenge: Potential relative decline in influence due to Elyseon's technological rise


________________


Dioclenis
________________


■ Characteristics and Role

Dioclenis is a force at the forefront of space exploration and scientific research. It continues to pioneer unknown dimensions and new planetary systems, and its research outcomes serve as critical infrastructure for other forces as well.

- Leader: Science Chancellor Nathan Corind
- Specialties: Space exploration, dimensional technology research, scientific research


■ Role at the Universal Federation Summit

At the first Universal Federation Summit, Nathan Corind played the most important role. Drawing on the lessons of the Apollon-Dominion War, he raised the danger of the "Thucydides Trap," scientifically pointing out the possibility that the current space order, centered on Granbell and Tyeria, could repeat the same mistakes.

Nathan also proposed converting the energy of conflict into joint space exploration. His proposal — "Should we not transcend our disputes and work together on space exploration?" — brought a momentary silence to the assembly and was received as a ray of hope.

He also made four concrete proposals: conclusion of a peace agreement, establishment of the Universal Economic Cooperation Organization (UECO), promotion of technology sharing, and expansion of cultural exchange.


■ Present

- Space Ranking: 5th
- Strengths: Space exploration, dimensional technology research and sharing
- Post-Summit Activity: Immersed in drafting a joint space exploration project with participation from all forces

Eresh
________________


A force possessing spiritual leadership and religious influence, with many civilizations building their order based on its teachings. Rather than economic or military power, it draws on ideological influence as its source, and at times exerts enough power to sway the political decisions of other forces.

- Space Ranking: 6th
- Strength: A unique power — religious and spiritual influence — that no other force possesses

Prokio

- Space Ranking: 7th

Roastar

- Space Ranking: 8th
