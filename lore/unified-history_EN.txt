── 1.1 Overview of M104 galaxy ──

Galaxy M104 (also known as the Sombrero Galaxy) is located approximately 31 million light years in the direction of the constellation Muromana.

It is a barred spiral galaxy. It has a diameter of about 50,000 light years and a mass equivalent to about 80% of the Milky Way galaxy.
With a supermassive black hole (1 billion times the mass of the sun) at the center as its core,
It has tens of billions of stars and an unknown number of planetary systems.
The halo region of M104 galaxy has low metal content and star formation activity has almost stopped.
The old stellar environment is widespread, and the E16 binary star system is located in this area.
Advanced civilizations are densely packed in the galactic disk, extending from the galactic center to the outer edge.
There is a tendency for the density of civilization to gradually decrease.
