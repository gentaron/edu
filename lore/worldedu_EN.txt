Prologue: Origin of the E16 star system (3500 AD/E1)
Geography and astronomical background of the E16 system
The E16 star system is a double binary star system located somewhere in the halo region of the M104 galaxy (Sombrero galaxy). The main star Ea16 (spectral type K2, mass 1.2 solar masses) and companion star Eb16 (spectral type M3, mass 0.4 solar masses) draw elliptical orbits at a distance of about 0.8 AU from each other, forming a stable gravitational field. This system is characterized by an old stellar environment where star formation activity has almost ceased, low metallicity, and few gas clouds and young stars. For this reason, E16 was considered a static environment more suitable for technological development and colonization than for resource extraction.

The E16 star system has seven planets, including the central planet "Symphony of Stars", and dozens of asteroid belts. Symphony of Stars is a terrestrial planet located within the habitable zone of Ea16, with an average surface temperature of 15℃ and gravity of 0.92G. The atmosphere was composed primarily of oxygen and nitrogen, with trace amounts of argon and biomarker gases, making it an ideal habitat for early settlers. The nearby planet Eros-7, on the other hand, has a harsher environment (1.1G gravity, thin oxygen atmosphere, and frequent electromagnetic storms), making it a challenge for early colonization.
Great immigration from Earth and the establishment of the E calendar
In the year 3500 A.D. (E0), Earth is facing overpopulation and resource depletion, and its survival is at risk due to climate change and geopolitical tensions. Around the same time, technological innovations in curvature navigation (warp propulsion) and quantum teleportation made intergalactic travel a reality. As a result, a diverse group of immigrants, consisting of the Fendrans (a technologically oriented Nordic group), the Archeans (an Asian group with excellent environmental adaptation), and the Poronpolos (an Oceanian group that values ​​cultural preservation), began immigrating via the intermediary route of the Andromeda → Leo → Sextance → Triangle → E16 binary star system.
The first group of immigrants to the E16 system landed on the Symphony of Stars in the year E0 (AD 3500), when the first group of 1,000 people descended on the Symphony of Stars. In the year E1 (3501 A.D.), the E calendar was established to match the rotation period of the central star Ea16 (44 hours 4 minutes, daytime 22 hours 2 minutes). The E calendar divides the year into 10-month "phases" based on the orbital period of Ea16 (approximately 320 Earth days). Each phase was 32 days long and subdivided into four "quarters" to streamline the work-rest cycle for immigrants. This calendar supported the strict timekeeping of the Symphony of the Stars and became the basis for the economic system of later corporate states.
Construction of Timur Shah and Persephone

Timur Shah, the leader of the emigrant group, was a proponent of the 10-dimensional theory of Horism and a thinker who combined mathematics, physics, and philosophy. He designed a virtual multiverse called Persephone to overcome Earth's VUCA (Variable, Uncertain, Complex, and Ambiguous) environment. Persephone, named after the "queen of the underworld" in Greek mythology, was an experimental platform for building a living space that transcends physical constraints. This system integrated bioengineering (adaptation to the environment through genetic modification) and dimensional technology (access to higher dimensions) and provided a means for immigrants to thrive in the harsh space environment.
Persephone's underlying technology relies on a Neurolink interface and a quantum computing core. Immigrants uploaded their consciousness to virtual space, keeping their bodies dormant while avoiding resource shortages and radiation environments. Timur runs social and economic simulations within Persephone based on the Khorezm framework (10-dimensional space-time model). This optimized the colony's governance structure and resource distribution, and suppressed the initial chaos. Timur's vision, that "the universe is an infinite canvas, and humanity paints in multiple dimensions", formed the cultural identity of E16.
Initial colonization of Eros-7 and introduction of exploitative organisms
Around the same time, a group of immigrants from the Symphony of Stars arrived on the outer planet Eros-7 in the E16 star system. Eros-7 was characterized by an unstable climate with a rocky surface, a thin oxygen atmosphere (12% oxygen concentration), and frequent electromagnetic storms. Early settlers built nanoshelters (radiation-resistant mobile homes) and biodomes to ensure survival. However, due to a lack of resources and a harsh environment, interaction with the Symphony of Stars was limited.

E16 system: fusion of technology and faith
Technological leap forward and the birth of the dimensional polar horizon
In the year 3580 AD (E80), the E16 system entered an era of technological enlightenment, with explosive advances in bioengineering and dimensional technology. The population of the central planet ``Symphony of Stars'' reached approximately 50 million people, and the prototype of the city-state ``Gigapolis'' was forming. This era was 80 years after Timur Shah's Persephone Project, and the technological base of the early immigrants had matured.
Bioengineering accelerates environmental adaptation through gene editing. Nanocell implants, which allow radiation resistance and longevity, became commonplace, and the immigrants adapted to E16's harsh space environment (high-energy particles and low-oxygen atmosphere). In terms of dimensional technology, ``Dimension Horizon'' has been developed, which applies black hole theory. This is a technology that manipulates quantum gravitational fields to access higher dimensions, creating a virtual multiverse through space holes (stable interdimensional portals). The Dimensional Polar Horizon became the basis for databases and energy generation to compensate for resource shortages, and dramatically developed the economy and society of E16.

Rise of the techno-religious movement

The success of Dimensional Polar Horizons inspired the techno-religious movement. This movement advocated a fusion of science and mysticism, and considered the dimensional polar horizon to be sacred as the "will of the universe." Believers view multidimensional space as the "realm of God" and pursue eternity through uploading consciousness. The movement's center, the Temple of Horizon, was built in Gigapolis, where rituals and meditations were held in virtual space. The temple was a giant dome with a quantum computing core that allowed believers to connect to the dimensional polar horizon and experience the higher dimensions as virtual avatars.
The leaders of the techno-religion took Temirtalon, a physicist who advocated psychedelic cosmology, as their spiritual pillar. Temirtalon expanded on Timur Shah's 10-dimensional Khorezm theory and envisioned a multidimensional political system that would become the prototype of the dimensional pyramid. Specifically, the Huns (8th dimension) envisioned a cooperative society of shared resources, the Sasanian (9th dimension) a network society of information governance, the Khorezm (10th dimension) a multiverse of consciousness integration, and the Timur (11th dimension) a transcendental society of complete free will. This four-layered high-dimensional world (Tier Ω), along with the virtual universe Persephone (Tier Σ), the E16 normal dimension (Tier Ε), and the AD2026 Earth (Tier Δ), make up all four layers of the dimensional pyramid. His book, The Dawn of Multidimensionality, became the intellectual foundation of E16 and was widely read among engineers and philosophers.
Evolution of Persephone: Primary Field
Persephone was rebuilt as a primary field under the guidance of Temirtalon. This was a virtual multiverse platform where users could project their consciousness into higher dimensional space and be freed from physical constraints. Primary Field integrates NeuroMatrix (a technology that connects consciousness and quantum computers) and Dimensional Grid (a multidimensional coordinate system) to enable economic activities, learning, and entertainment in virtual space. For example, immigrants simulated food production on virtual farms and saved real resources.

Qualia Core (a technology that digitizes emotions and consciousness) was essential to the operation of the Primary Field. This gives users a more realistic experience in a virtual space and strengthens community cohesion. However, this dependence on advanced technology later led to the emergence of a hierarchical society and disparity in the corporate state.
Establishment of education and social systems
Due to the rapid population growth, schools for 6–14 year olds were established. These schools taught bioengineering (genetic design), dimensional theory (space hole manipulation), survival techniques (radiation protection), and developed adaptability in the harsh space environment. Separate e-School is an online education system based on a credit economy, where students purchase learning modules and on-the-job training using n-tokens (digital currency). Those with excellent grades were promised promotion to the higher echelons of the A-registration system, forming the elite class of the corporate state.
This educational system laid the foundation for the later corporatum publica (corporate state) and provided a source of engineers and managers. However, lower-class immigrants, who had difficulty acquiring n-tokens, had limited educational opportunities and began to experience a decline in social mobility.
Eros-7: Exploited creatures and the emergence of a female-led society
Isolation and unique evolution
At the same time, Eros-7 was an isolated colony with limited interaction with the Symphony of Stars as an outer planet in the E16 star system. Survival was extremely difficult due to electromagnetic storms and a thin oxygen atmosphere (12% oxygen concentration). Immigrants strengthened biodomes and nanoshelters and built an underground city called "Neon Crater." The Neon Crater was illuminated with bioluminescent materials derived from exploited organisms and became the prototype for the later Neon Crater Palace.
Eros-7's society evolved around Squeezing Organisms introduced through interstellar trade. These parasites absorbed the host's sexual energy and suppressed male reproductive function, while females were less affected due to genetic resistance. This biological sex difference had a dramatic impact on social structure and laid the foundation for women to occupy leadership positions.

Female leader Elana Ceres, successor to Lilith Vane, developed the prototype for exploitative technology by bioengineering exploitative organisms. Specifically, aphrodisiac gases (chemicals that induce sexual arousal) and exploitative leeches (parasites that efficiently extract energy) were put into practical use. These technologies extract male sexual energy and convert it into electricity in a bioreactor, alleviating Eros-7's energy crisis. The men were managed as energy workers and connected to an exploitation hill in an underground facility in Neon Crater.
Exploitative techniques supported Eros-7's economy while lowering men's social status. Women developed biocosmetics (promoting skin regeneration) and bioweapons (gas weapons that paralyze the nervous system) using the secretions of exploited organisms to strengthen the defense power of the colony. Elana Seles establishes a Women's Council to oversee the management and distribution of exploitative technologies. The Council introduced a proto-registry that registered men as 'resource providers', laying the foundations for the later sperm registry.
Formation of a women-led society
Due to the gendered influence of exploitative organisms, Eros-7 formed an early Matrical society.女性は、搾取技術の管理者として経済・政治的権力を掌握。 Men were locked into labor and energy supply roles, limiting their social mobility. The Neon Crater Palace became the residence and governing center of the female leader, and grew to become a cultural symbol of Eros-7, a magnificent structure glowing with the bioluminescence of the exploited creatures.
Culturally, exploitation rituals developed, in which women used exploitation leeches to extract energy from men, symbolizing community cohesion. The Festival of Flare, a celebration of exploitative technology with bioluminescent dance and music, reinforced Eros-7's identity. However, as men became increasingly dissatisfied, a small underground movement began to emerge.

The economic scale of Gigapolis reached 5 quintillion tokens per year, and the interstellar communications provided by Dimension Horizon and the production revolution provided by nanofabricators propelled the E16 system to become the economic center of the M104 galaxy.

Pax Lombardica symbolized peace on the surface due to corporate prosperity, but behind the scenes, the A-register system (155-level class system) exacerbated inequality. High class members (A1-A30) enjoy a luxurious life in the sky city of megatowers and manage virtual assets within the primary field. On the other hand, those in the lower classes (A100–A155) were forced to do harsh labor in underground living quarters, and their access to education and healthcare was restricted due to the lack of n-tokens. At Gigapolis' Central Tower, the A rank promotion exam has become stricter, and the success rate has dropped to less than 1%. The dissatisfaction of the lower classes led to increased activities of the Shadow Rebellion, and underground anti-regime meetings became more frequent.
Culturally, the battle for warriors in the Neon Colosseum reached its peak, symbolizing civic entertainment and the hope of ascending the hierarchy. Warriors made full use of nano armor and plasma blades and competed in battles that combined virtual and reality, and the audience rating exceeded 90%. While the event encouraged individual agency, it also served as propaganda to legitimize the domination of the corporate state.

On the other hand, those in the lower classes (A100–A155) were bound to harsh labor in underground living quarters, and had almost no access to education or medical care due to the lack of n-tokens. Shadow Rebellion expanded its power with the success of Alpha Cain's Gigapolis Liberation Battle (E318) and strengthened its guerrilla activities using nanohacking. Around E320, an incident occurred that temporarily shut down Gigapolis's energy grid, calling into question Corporatum Publica's ability to govern.
The rise of the Neo-Clan Alliance and the search for decentralized governance
The Neo-Clan Alliance continued Alpha Cain's ideals and sought to spread the model of decentralized governance throughout Gigapolis. In E320–E340, local clans (autonomous communities) form in the lower levels of the Megatower and on surrounding planets to build local versions of the Quantum Finance Core. This began the regional circulation of n-tokens and partially realized economic autonomy for the lower class. Clan leaders leverage primary fields and coordinate policies in virtual meetings. The Clan Forum (founded in E325) was held in the basement of Gigapolis and became a platform for the voices of the lower classes to be heard by the corporate state.
However, Corporatum Publica considers the rise of the Neo-Clan Alliance to be a threat and has deployed a nano-drone surveillance network. In E330, several leaders of the Clan Forum were arrested under the pretext of "anti-regime activities," and riots broke out in underground living quarters. In the Neon Colosseum, warrior battles were used as political propaganda, reinforcing the show that legitimized the corporate state's control. The new rules for the warrior battle introduce a dimensional arena (an infinite battle environment in virtual space), and the warriors' nano armor and plasma blades are strengthened with dimensional technology. Although the audience rating reached 95%, the dissatisfaction of the lower class people did not subside, and this became a remote cause of the later slime crisis (E380).

However, health problems for male workers continued, with cases of energy depletion increasing by 10%. The Abyss Chamber (underground exploitation facility) was strictly controlled with surveillance cameras and nano-drones, making escape nearly impossible.
Expansion of the Shadow Union and signs of civil war
The dissatisfaction of male workers encouraged the growth of shadow unions. Around E320, Shadow Union secretly learned ZAMLT's nanohacking technology and sabotaged the control system of the exploited energy core. In E330, in the "Leech Destruction Incident" that occurred in the Abyss Chamber, 1000 exploitation leeches were destroyed and the energy supply temporarily decreased by 20%. The Matrical Council dispersed an anti-exploitation agent and suppressed the rebellion, but the leader of the Shadow Union, Garo (later an ally of Ayaka Rin), organized a secret society underground. Garo took advantage of Eros-7's isolation to expand his resistance movement while avoiding contact with the Symphony of Stars.
Culturally, the Festival of Flair strengthens the authority of Matrical society. At a festival held in the palace's hologram dome in E340, a performance using exploitative tentacles was broadcast across the galaxy, demonstrating Eros-7's technological prowess. However, Shadow Union spread anti-establishment messages in holograms during the festival, calling for the liberation of men. This movement became the basis for the later male backlash during the Slime Crisis (E380).
Influence of the Neo-Clan Alliance and signs of reform

Around the year E350, the ideals of the Neo-Clan Alliance from the E16 system spread to Eros-7. The Matrical Council pilots a regional energy cooperative, inspired by the Neo-Clan Alliance's decentralized governance model. A portion of the exploited energy core was distributed to local communities, giving male workers limited autonomy. Although this reform temporarily suppressed the activities of the Shadow Union, it was far from achieving complete gender equality, and the resistance movement centered on Garo maintained its power underground. In E360, a bioart movement sprouted on Eros-7, with anti-establishment art using the secretions of exploited creatures secretly exhibited in the Abyss Chamber.
Contrast and mutual influence between E16 star system and Eros-7
The Fifth Prosperity of the E16 system, from E318–E370, was a period of conflict between the dominance of the Corporatum Publica and the resistance of the Neo-Clan Alliance. Gigapolis' neon colosseum and dimensional art movement symbolize the duality of prosperity and dissatisfaction. On the other hand, in Eros-7's Matrical society, the militarization of exploitation technology and the resistance of the Shadow Union conflict. Both planets were indirectly linked through the n-token economy and the dimensional polar horizon, with Eros-7's exploitative gas weapons supplied to the military enterprises of Gigapolis. Conversely, E16's nanomedicine technology contributed to the improvement of Eros-7's exploitative bacteria.
However, E16's A-registration system and Eros-7's Matrical Code strengthen oppression in different ways. The Battle of the Warriors and the Festival of Flare became sites of power and resistance on their respective planets, setting the stage for the later Slime Crisis and the Apollo-Dominion War. The Neo-Clan Alliance's decentralized governance philosophy sowed the seeds of change on both planets and became the foundation for what would become the Galactic Consortium (E500).

The Apollo-Dominion War (3870–3885 AD/E370–E385) was a major conflict between the E16 star system and the M104 galaxy's military nation groups. The war began with a conflict over control of a dimensional energy mine, and E16's dimensional weapon (a mass-destroying weapon that utilizes a space hole) was victorious. The war reorganized the Galactic Alliance and elevated the E16 system to become the dominant force in the M104 galaxy. Furthermore, the occupation of Gigapolis by Evatron in E400 is a separate event from the end of the war.
The World Federation Summit (4024 AD/524 E) was held in the Central Tower of Gigapolis and proposed a cooperative framework to avoid the Thucydides Trap. Representatives of a galactic consortium sign an interstellar peace treaty, pledging resource sharing and technological transparency. Summit leveraged the heroic images of Layla and Ayaka to appeal to the galaxy for the unification of E16.
Eros-7: Isolation and the birth of reform
Eros-7 remained isolated during the war, specializing in exploitative technology for military use. Exploitation plasma munitions were exported as defensive weapons against Dominion Confederate fleets, increasing Eros-7's economic power. However, the arrival of Ayaka Lin (4025 AD/525 E) shook the Matrical Order. Along with Garo and Zena, she organizes the Matrical Reform movement, demanding the abolition of exploitation sessions and the restoration of men's citizenship. Influenced by the Summit's ideals, Eros-7 established a joint council of men and women and began the transition to an equal society.

AURALIS — About
Where Light and Sound Become Eternal
AURALIS Collective is an art group that has grown together with the history of the E16 star system.

Around the year E270, the predecessor organization "AURALIS Proto" was launched under the cultural blessings of the Diana (first Wonder Woman) era. Around E290, the first generation was formally organized around Kate Patton and Lillie Ardent. The Golden Age of Seria (E335-E370) reached its peak with Fermi music and the peak of the n-token economy, but was dismantled due to Evatron's cultural oppression. Kate's first generation and Lillie's first generation were captured and their whereabouts are unknown, and only Leila Villers Nova survived due to cryopreservation.
After E514, reconstruction began under the leadership of Leila, and the second generation was officially launched around E522. Kate Patton (new generation), Lillie Ardent (new generation), Leila Mina, Eureka, Ernest, and Ninny Offenbach continue to work with the mission of ``making light and sound eternal.''
In E525, he launched Liminal Forge, and uses Temporal Broadcast, which combines Apolonium (a dimensional catalyst material originating from the Apollo civilization) and Dimension Horizon technology, to broadcast the art of E528 on the Internet in 2026 AD. It is not physical time travel, but ``time irradiation'' of information, consciousness, and art.
Genesis Vault — About
The Genesis Vault is Mina Eureka Ernest's "Vault of Thoughts" that appeared on Earth in 2026 AD.
"Genesis" is a archive set in Earth 2026, the distant origin of the E16 civilization. "Vault" is Mina's thought archive.
Mina was born in E499 in the former Dominion colony "Nostalgia". Her father is an engineer and her mother is a historical recorder. When she was a child, her parents told her about the ``former glory of Gigapolis'' - the legend of Celia's golden age - which became the origin of her spiritual hometown. In 509 E5, when she was 10 years old, Nostalgia was nearly destroyed by Alpha Venom's attack, and she escaped on a refugee ship. The flash of the ``gravity collapse warhead'' became an ``awakening to strategy.''

The flash of the ``gravity collapse warhead'' became an ``awakening to strategy.''

At the age of 15, he entered the AI ​​Department of Lorenzio International University on the academic city planet "Biblio". Based on the belief that "history is driven by emotions, but there are parts that can be predicted using mathematical formulas," he researches models for predicting the collapse of civilization. After graduating at the age of 22, he began wandering as a backpacker with the idea of ​​"Veni, vidi, vici" in mind. ``Mina's Space Rules Strategy Log'' became the prototype for the later Genesis Vault.
At the World Federation Summit in E524, he first came into contact with information about the year 2026 AD, and realized that ``this era is the distant origin of our civilization,'' which led to the creation of Liminal Forge. From the perspective of Earth, Mina is recognized as an "AI blogger," but this is technically accurate since her consciousness is output through an AI system.
As of E528, Genesis Vault has exceeded 2,000 volumes. Mina continues to broadcast commentary sampling information from Earth 2026 on themes such as AI, modern society, games, and self-development, with an E16 interpretation.
