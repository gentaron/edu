## Prologue: A Question Born in a Voiceless Era

E270. The gigapolis sprawling across the western continent of the Symphony of Stars was in chaos as the long reign of the Lombardia Empire drew to a close.

Commerce was in disarray, culture was fragmented, and people were losing their sense of "meaning." Information overflowed, yet there was no "voice" that could reach anyone's soul.

In that era, Kate Patton lived.

She was neither a musician nor a revolutionary. She was simply a person who carried a question.

*Why is there no expression on this star that truly "reaches" people?*

That question was the beginning of everything.

---

## Chapter 1: A Small Founding, E270

AURALIS was not an "organization" from the start.

Kate met her ally Lillie Ardent in a small gathering space in a narrow alley of the gigapolis, its walls covered in musical note graffiti. There, a handful of artists who shared the same question had gathered. Singers, storytellers, visual artists, and those who understood silence.

Kate and Lillie recognized each other instinctively — this is the one.

If Lillie was the flame of emotion, Kate was the mold that gave that flame form. Lillie possessed a passion that could set the world ablaze, and Kate continuously built structures to ensure that passion would never burn out.

E270. They named their small group "AURALIS."

"I just want to give wings to people's ears. That's all I want to do."

At that point in E270, AURALIS was a group of fewer than five. They had no grand ambitions. They were simply people who refused to give up on expression in a world that had given up.

---

## Chapter 2: The End of the Empire and the Formal Birth of a Voice, E290

E290. The era when the aftershocks of the Lombardia Empire had faded and the ZAMLT Union — a coalition of over one hundred million corporations — was beginning to rise. Amidst the confusion of shifting power dynamics, AURALIS was formally organized as "Phase 1."

Kate did not like being called a leader. She allowed herself to be called "the Architect."

When Lillie stood on stage with everything she had, Kate worked behind the scenes, composing musical structures, articulating the group's philosophy, and continually asking "why are we here?" whenever members lost their way.

AURALIS's philosophy was recorded in Kate's words in E290:

*"Be a presence that delivers the emotion born in a fleeting moment across time."*

"Make Light and Sound Eternal" — these words became the core of AURALIS.

It was not a slogan. It was Kate's faith.

---

## Chapter 3: A New Voice in the Storm, circa E325

Around E320, an event shook the gigapolis. Alpha Kane's rebellion.

In the midst of that chaos, one woman awakened. Layla Virel Nova — the being who would later be called "Pink Voltage."

Around E325, Layla joined AURALIS.

When Kate first saw Layla, she reportedly said after a long silence:

*"You are the storm itself. AURALIS needs the storm."*

With Layla's addition, AURALIS's music transformed. From an expression that "delivered" to an expression that "shook." Kate did not fear the change. Rather, she enjoyed redesigning the architecture.

The relationship between Layla and Kate was one of being at opposite poles while needing each other. When Layla filled the space with cosmic energy, Kate quietly served as gravity, ensuring that energy would not scatter.

---

## Chapter 4: The Golden Age — Beneath Celia's Sky, E335–E370

E335. Celia Dominicus defeated Alpha Kane and brought a golden age to the gigapolis. The city's name gained new brilliance as "Cerinopolis," Fermi music filled the streets, and the nToken economy gave culture economic wings.

The peak of AURALIS Phase 1 began here.

Kate called this era "the Age of Proof."

AURALIS's music flowed everywhere in the gigapolis. In alleyways, in plazas, on factory walls. People danced, cried, laughed, and remembered emotions they had forgotten. Expression had "reached" them.

Around this time, Kate reportedly had a habit of writing once a year in her notebook:

*"Did it reach someone this year? If it did, I can do it again next year."*

It was a simple motivation. But that motivation never wavered from E270 onward.

Lillie burned on stage, Layla drew the cosmos closer, and each member honed their craft. And Kate continued to design and continued to ask questions.

The thirty-five years from E335 to E370. For AURALIS Phase 1, it was a golden age that felt eternal.

---

## Chapter 5: The Shadow of Dominion and the Approaching End, E390–E400

From E370, the winds of change shifted.

The onset of the Dominion era. The long prelude to the Apollo-Dominion War. Cracks in Celia's regime.

Around E390, Kate reportedly said quietly to Lillie:

*"A storm is coming. But we know that the music before the storm is the most beautiful."*

AURALIS never stopped expressing themselves, until the very end.

E400. Evatron seized control of the gigapolis. The city was renamed "Evapolis," and a new color of domination was painted over everything.

AURALIS Phase 1 was ordered to disband.

Kate Patton the First and Lillie Ardent the First were arrested. Their subsequent fate was erased from the records.

Only Layla survived, in the form of cryopreservation (cryopreserve). As the last inheritor of the "light and sound" that Kate and Lillie had done everything to protect.

---

## Epilogue: The Question Continues

As of E528, AURALIS Phase 2 is active.

A new Kate Patton, a new Lillie Ardent, the Layla resurrected from cryopreservation, Mina Eureka Ernest, and Ninie Offenbach. They continue to broadcast across dimensions through the Liminal Forge.

When Mina read the records of the first Kate, she reportedly wrote:

*"She did not seek fame. She continued to design. She continued to question. With that alone, for over 130 years, the light called AURALIS has not gone out."*

*"There is a saying: 'I came, I saw, I conquered.' But Kate was a little different."*

*"I came, I listened, I continued."*

*"That, I believe, was how the first Kate lived."*

## Prologue: The Mystery in the Mirror

E275. In the back alleys of the Abrivo district in the lower sector of the gigapolis, there was a mirror.

Before that mirror stood a figure. Long golden curls. Translucently white skin. High cheekbones and softly curved lips. Possessing an endlessly feminine silhouette, yet somewhere in the bone structure, there dwelled an edge that could not be explained as merely female.

Lily Steiner. The being who would later become a legend of AURALIS was, at this time, still a nameless artist.

*"Who am I?"*

She had possessed the habit of asking this question toward the mirror since childhood. But the mirror always returned the same thing. A beautiful, ambiguous existence that refused definition.

That ambiguity was the source of her expression.

---

## Chapter 1: The Meeting of Flames, E270

Lily Steiner met Kate Patton in E270.

In a small gathering space in a narrow alley of the gigapolis, its walls covered in musical note graffiti. There, a handful of artists who shared the same question had gathered. Singers, storytellers, visual artists, and those who understood silence.

That night, Lily sang. Without planning or preparation, as if drawn by the atmosphere of the moment, she let her voice rise from the depths of her abdomen.

When the song ended, no one clapped. Everyone had held their breath.

Only Kate quietly stood and spoke:

*"You are a flame."*

Lily laughed. "Flames burn out."

*"They do," Kate answered. "That is why I will build the structure so that you don't burn out."*

From that night on, the two were together.

If Lily was the flame of emotion, Kate was the mold that gave that flame form. Lily possessed a passion that could shake the world, and Kate continued to design so that passion would never burn out.

E270. They named their small group "AURALIS."

---

## Chapter 2: The Day Ambiguous Beauty Became a Weapon

Contemporary records describe Lily Steiner's appearance as follows:

*"When she steps onto the stage, the audience is first confused. Then, they cannot look away. And finally, they understand. She is singing all boundaries."*

On the small AURALIS stages that began in the back alleys of the Abrivo district, Lily's appearance often provoked controversy. Was she female? Male? The question itself was part of her expression.

Lily never resolved that question.

Her makeup and costumes were deliberately chosen to disrupt boundaries. The hem of her skirt swept the cobblestones, her chest adorned with ornaments, yet her voice was low, her gaze sharp, and her presence on stage simultaneously radiated "softness" and "pressure."

Kate, observing Lily's strategy of expression, said quietly:

*"You are placing the question itself on stage. Not giving an answer is your answer."*

Lily repeated those words before the mirror and, for the first time, recognized her ambiguity as a "weapon."

---

## Chapter 3: Storm and Ice — The Era with Layla, E325

Around E325, a woman joined AURALIS. Layla Virel Nova.

When Lily first saw her, she intuited immediately. *This person is a different kind of flame from mine.*

If Lily's flame was the heat of emotion, Layla's flame was cosmic energy. The two flames did not repel each other; rather, they amplified one another.

But Layla had a curious habit that no other member possessed.

She would disappear periodically.

At first, it was said that she "went on a journey." But in reality, Layla was entering cold sleep (cryosleep).

Only Lily was the first to know this fact.

*"Why do you sleep?"*

One night, in the white room of the facility, Lily asked Layla just before she was about to enter her case.

Layla paused for a moment before answering. "My body is not made to wear out at a normal rate. By sleeping, I can stay here much longer."

*"Will you come back?"*

"Absolutely."

Lily believed those words. And indeed, Layla slept many times and returned many times. After three months. After six months. After a year.

Each time Layla awoke with the exact same face as before she slept. She did not age. She carried no fatigue. As if time itself was avoiding her.

Each time Layla awakened, Lily began saying this:

*"Welcome back. You caught up with me again."*

It was a joke, and yet it was not. Every time Layla awakened, Lily had aged a little more.

---

## Chapter 4: The Golden Age — Beneath Celia's Blazing Sky, E335–E370

E335. Celia Dominicus defeated Alpha Kane and brought a golden age to the gigapolis.

For AURALIS Phase 1, this was the true peak.

During this era, Lily felt something had changed on stage. It was not the audience that had changed, but rather "what the audience needed" that had shifted, she reportedly said.

*"In the past, people wanted to see beautiful things. But now, there is something they want to reach them. Something they want to receive. They want something put into their souls."*

Lily's expression was most sharpened during the Celia golden age. Her ambiguous appearance was now spoken of as "myth," and the name Lily Steiner on the AURALIS stage became known in every district of the gigapolis.

At her performances, there were those who wept, those who danced, and those who stood transfixed. No two people received it the same way. That was Lily's pride.

*"It doesn't matter who I am. What you felt — that is the answer."*

Behind the scenes, Kate continued to support Lily's structure, while Layla, having undergone several short cold sleeps in the interim, poured cosmic energy onto the stage each time she awakened. AURALIS operated through the different forms of permanence held by each of the three.

Lily's permanence was "the flame itself." The act of burning. That was her reason for existing.

---

## Chapter 5: The Last Song Before Dawn, E390–E400

From E370, the color of the era began to shift.

The shadow of the Dominion era. The prelude to the Apollo-Dominion War. Cracks in Celia's regime.

Lily felt these changes more sensitively on her skin than anyone else. The eyes of the audience had changed. Behind the smiles lay unease. In the applause, there was urgency.

Around E390. Layla entered cold sleep once more. This time, Lily said nothing. She only held Layla's hand once, just before she fell asleep.

"It might be longer than usual," Layla said.

*"I know."*

Lily said only that, and left the facility.

E400. Evatron seized control of the gigapolis.

That night, after their final stage, Lily Steiner and Kate Patton were arrested in front of the facility. The records contain nothing more. No reason, no destination, no subsequent fate.

The only certain thing is that, just before her arrest, Lily reportedly said this to the few people present:

*"AURALIS will not disappear. Even if the flame sleeps, the ashes remain. From the ashes, it will burn again."*

Those who passed on those words later conveyed them to Layla when she awakened.

---

## Epilogue: The Reason the Name is Carried On

E528. In AURALIS Phase 2, there exists a being who carries the name "Lillie Ardent."

The name is "Lillie Ardent," not "Lillie Steiner." Yet within that name, Steiner's flame certainly dwells.

AURALIS has a system of name inheritance. Those who possess the same characteristics receive the name. The name Lillie Ardent is carried on because its bearer is "the flame of emotion."

Lily Steiner transformed a single characteristic into a name and crossed eras.

After her resurrection in Phase 2, when Layla first saw the new generation's Lillie Ardent, she reportedly said after a long silence:

*"I see her in your eyes."*

The new-generation Lillie could not understand the meaning of those words for a while. But when she read through AURALIS's records, she finally understood. Layla, having repeated cold sleep and awakening dozens of times, still remembered the eyes of the person who had said "welcome back" to her each time, even now, over a hundred years later.

Mina later recorded this about Steiner in the Genesis Vault:

*"She gave no answers. She expressed the question itself. She chose what she made people feel over who she was."*

*"Even if the flame sleeps, it does not go out. Through Layla's cold sleep, Lily may have known that better than anyone."*

*"Lily Steiner. Even if that name is buried in history, as long as there is a flame on the AURALIS stage, she is there."*
